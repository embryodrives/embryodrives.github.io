Embryo

A seed for change in the automotive world.


Environmentally Friendly

The car built through this project will be powered by seawater. Yes, seawater. For this reason, it will produce no emissions and be friendlier to the envrionment.

Design is on the agenda

This car is designed to be good to look at and functional. Our goal is to show to car companies that such a thing is possible, and that ugly, incomprehensible cars should be a thing of the past.

Built for India

We aim to design the car for a trip across India, from Kanyakummari to Kashmir. It will face hot temperatures, cold temperatures, deserts, streams, jungles, and snow. This will be the ultimate testing ground for the project.
